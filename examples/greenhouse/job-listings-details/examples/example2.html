What you’ll do

As a  Product Manager - Intern , you will gain hands-on experience contributing to innovative product initiatives and collaborating with cross-functional teams. This role is ideal for someone passionate about technology, user experience, and growth strategies. You’ll have the opportunity to make a meaningful impact, learning from a team of experienced professionals while developing skills in product management and growth strategies.
 
Responsibilities
  • Collaborate with product, engineering, design, and marketing teams to build delightful and intuitive product experiences.
  • Assist in identifying and prioritizing product improvement opportunities using data-driven insights.
  • Support the development and execution of product-led growth experiments to enhance user acquisition, activation, retention, and revenue.
  • Contribute to user research initiatives to better understand customer needs and pain points.
  • Help track and analyze key growth and user engagement metrics, presenting findings to stakeholders.
  • Work on A/B testing and optimization strategies to improve conversion rates across the user journey.

  • Interest in growth strategies, A/B testing, and product-led growth.
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
